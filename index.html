Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 19, 2025

Time Assistant ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from users.

Ad Services

We use Google AdMob to serve ads in our app. AdMob may collect certain information such as your device ID, advertising ID (AAID), and usage data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads. This data is handled according to Google's Privacy Policy.

Permissions Used

Time Assistant uses the following permissions for its core functionality:

  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: To reschedule notifications after device restart.
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM: To trigger reminders at precise times.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: To support background reminders and notifications.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To display reminders and task alerts.
  • INTERNET / NETWORK STATE: Required to load ads from Google AdMob.

Data Security

We do not collect or store any personal information. Any data used for ads is managed by Google AdMob under their privacy and security practices.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
